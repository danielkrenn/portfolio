About Me

With over 25 years in the industry, I've had the privilege of working on everything from beloved AAA franchises like the Spyro series and Ty the Tasmanian Tiger, to pioneering MMORPGs, VR experiences for global brands, blockchain games, and high-precision simulation tools for civil engineering. From low-level engine systems and custom in-house engines in C++ to architecting complex multiplayer, AI, and physics-driven experiences in Unity, I've thrived on turning ambitious ideas into polished, performant realities that players love.

My career has spanned massive team environments and scrappy indie/crypto projects, giving me deep expertise in 3D math, physics, tooling, and a wide range of languages and platforms. Whether optimizing frame rates under pressure, building mission editors and developer tools, integrating emerging tech like blockchain or VR hardware, or mentoring the next generation of developers, I’m driven by the thrill of solving hard problems and shipping games/simulations that leave a lasting impression.

Today, I’m as excited as ever about what’s next — whether pushing the boundaries of Unity development, building immersive simulations, or joining a passionate team on something new and ambitious. I bring not just decades of battle-tested knowledge, but genuine enthusiasm for collaboration and innovation.
