About Me

With over 25 years in the industry, I've had the privilege of working on everything from beloved AAA franchises like the Spyro series and Ty the Tasmanian Tiger, to pioneering MMORPGs, VR experiences for global brands, blockchain games, and high-precision simulation tools for civil engineering. From low-level engine systems and custom in-house engines in C++ to architecting complex multiplayer, AI, and physics-driven experiences in Unity, I've thrived on turning ambitious ideas into polished, performant realities that players love.

My career has spanned massive team environments and scrappy indie/crypto projects, giving me deep expertise in 3D math, physics, tooling, and a wide range of languages and platforms. I have a proven track record in performance optimization, complex bug resolution, and delivering stable, high-quality systems under tight deadlines. Whether building mission editors, integrating emerging tech, or mentoring developers, I’m driven by the thrill of solving hard problems and shipping games/simulations that leave a lasting impression.

Currently available for contract or full-time senior/lead roles (Brisbane or fully remote). Particularly interested in projects involving complex systems, multiplayer, AI, VR/AR, simulation, or innovative gameplay mechanics.
